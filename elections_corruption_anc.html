ANC Promises on Corruption

  • Pursue action against companies involved in bid rigging, price fixing and corruption in past and current infrastructure build programmes
  • Use the competition authorities, take up civil cliams and call on the National prosecuting Authority to investigate criminally-liable behaviour
  • Prohibit public servants and public representatives from doing business with the state, and develop measures to identify and prosecute corrupt actions by public servants and others
  • Establish a centralised process with stakeholder representation to adjudicate on major tenders in all spheres of govermnet
  • Work with the chief procurement official, whose main functions will be to check on pricing and cost effectiveness, and ensure transparency, adherence to procedures and fairness
